    Scientific Education

    2018. Associate Professor at VNUHCM-University of Science

    2013. Ph.D in Nuclear Physics: VNUHCM-University of Science

    2008. Master of Nuclear Physics, VNUHCM-University of Science

    2004. Bachelor of Physics, VNUHCM-University of Science

    Academic & Research Experience

    Scientific Research

    9 projects of scientific research

    Research Skills/Interests

    - Analysis of naturally occurring radionuclide in environmental samples.

    - Alpha spectroscopy (Radiochemistry) using PIPS semiconductor detector.

    - Gamma spectroscopy (NaI and HpGe detector).

    - Gross alpha / beta counting systems.

    - Radon (Noble gas) monitoring using RAD7 and track detectors (CR-39,…).
