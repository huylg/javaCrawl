    HA Thuc Chi Nhan, PhD

    Associate Professor,
    Fundamental Materials Science Laboratory
    Faculty of Materials Science & Technology
    University of Science, VNUHCM
    Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
    Tel: (+84) 906 628 356
    Email:htcnhan@hcmus.edu.vn

    Scientific Education

    • 2004: B.S. In Physical Chemistry, University of Science – National University of HoChiMinh City, Vietnam
    • 2005: M.S. in Physico-chemistry of Polymer Materials - University of Maine, Le Mans City, France, 2005
    • 2008: Ph.D. in Polymer and Composite Materials, University of Savoie, Chambéry City, France

    Academic & Research Experience

    Research Skills/Interests:
    • Extraction/Purification and synthesis of nanomaterials from waste natural renewable source,
    • Elaboration of high performant engineering polymer composites and nanocomposites reinforced by 1D, 2D and 3D nano-materials,
    • Synthesis/Elaboration of biodegradable/compatible polymer blends based on starch and cellulose – Application in eco-friendly and biomedical plastic materials,
    • Study on elaboration of biocompatible polymer composite/nanocomposite based on BioSource polymers for biomedical applications (wound healing dressing, artificial skin, artificial bone and scaffold)
    • Study on the antibacterial of functional polymer composite/nanocomposites – Application of agricultural preservative packaging materials, household plastics, antibacterial fabrics, food packaging, medical supplies, coating paint
    • Elaboration of polymer composite or polymer nanocomposite based on biowaste agricultural source and recycled plastic - Application for engineering materials in construction field
    • Synthesis of nanostructured materials from agricultural / industrial waste products - Applied as absorbent materials for heavy metals and organic impurities in polluted water environment
    • Synthesis of nanomaterials for environmental treatment, agricultural or cosmetic materials
    • Study on the polymer masterbatch for plastic additives field
