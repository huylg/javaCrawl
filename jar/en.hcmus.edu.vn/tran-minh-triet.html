    Associate Professor,
    Vice President,
    Deputy Head of Artificial Intelligence Lab
    Head of Software Engineering Lab
    University of Science, VNUHCM
    Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
    Tel: (+84) 287 3089 899
    Email:tmtriet@hcmus.edu.vn

    Scientific Education

    • 2005-2009: Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science, University of Science, VNU-HCM, Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam
    • 2002-2005: Master of Science in Computer Science, University of Science, VNU-HCM, Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam
    • 1997-2001: Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, University of Science, VNU-HCM, Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam

    Academic & Research Experience

    Research Interests

    • Multimedia, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision
    • Software Engineering and Human-Computer Interaction

    • Director of Provincial Programming Contest of ICPC (Southern Vietnam) from 2015 to 2019.
