    VNUHCM-University of Science holds the ceremony to award Ho Chi Minh City Medals and VNU-HCM Campaign Medals for 8 professors from Roskilde University

    01 Feb,2018

    Awarding the HCM City Badge and the VNUHCM Memorial Medal to 8 Danish professors

    On the occasion of the 15th anniversary between Roskilde University, Denmark and VNUHCM - University of Science, to continue supporting and expanding the cooperation between the two sides, VNUHCM - University of Science held The Awarding Ceremony of HCMC Badge and VNUHCM Memorial Medal to 8 Danish Professors: Erik Thulstrup, Fritz Duus, Poul Erik Hansen, Jens Spanget Larsen, Ole Vang, Torben Lund, John Mortensen and Soren Hvidt from Roskilde University, Denmark.

    The 8 professors have greatly contributed  to postgraduate training and scientific research for Vietnam through the ENRECA project funded by DAINIDA (Denmark). In recognition of their great contributions, the HCM City People's Committee decided to award the HCMC Badge as the city's most prestigious award; and the President of VNUHCM also decided to award the VNUHCM memorial medal to the 8 professors.

    Attending the ceremony, on the Vietnamese side, there were Ms. Nguyen Thi Thu - Vice Chairman of the HCMC People's Committee and the Board of management from University of Science: Prof.Dr. Tran Linh Thuoc - Rector, Assoc.Prof.Dr. Tran Le Quan - Vice Rector and Prof.Dr. Chau Van Tao - Vice Rector. On behalf of the Denmark side, there were Mr. Morten Pristed - Education and Health Advisor, Embassy of Denmark; Prof. Poul Erik Hansen - Roskilde University; Prof. Fritz Duus; Prof. Jens Spanget-Larsen with his spouse Marie Louise Spanget-Larsen, Prof. Ole Vang, Prof. Torben Lund.

    To commemorate the fruitful cooperation, as well as opening up new and more comprehensive relationships in the future, the University of Science and the University of Roskilde have signed a memorandum of cooperation and exchange in training and scientific research.

     

    Prof. Dr. Tran Linh Thuoc, Rector of the University of Science (left) and Prof. Poul Erik Hansen - Representative of Roskilde University signing the MOU

     

    Speaking at the ceremony, Prof. Dr. Tran Linh Thuoc, Rector of the University of Science, thanked the professors from Roskilde University for their special affection for Vietnamese students in general and students of University of Science in particular.

    Sharing his thoughts at the ceremony, Prof. Poul Erik Hansen said: "The ENRECA project not only produces significant scientific results, but also creates opportunities for all of us to meet and exchange with different cultures and different thingkings. This is truly a wonderful experience and my colleagues and I hope that this cooperation will go on even after the first project has ended. We hope that the his project will continue in the future.”

     

    - Professor Fritz Duus, who supervised 3 PhD and 11 master’s students in the program. He was the program’s co-ordinator from 2009 to 2011.

    - Professor Poul Erik Hansen, who contributed to the continuous development of our partnership beyond the ENRECA program. He supervised 3 PhD and 5 master’s students in the program, and another 1 PhD and 2 master’s students in Viet Nam after the program.

    - Professor Jens Spanget-Larsen, who supervised 2 PhD and 2 master’s students in the program.

    - Professor Ole Vang, who supervised 1 PhD and 8 master’s students in the program. He also contributed to expanding research at the Faculty of Biology and the Faculty of Chemistry into new areas.

    - Professor Torben Lund, who supervised 1 PhD and 9 master’s student in the program. After the program had ended, he supervised another PhD student from Viet Nam, and remains active in promoting collaboration between the two universities.

    - And Professor John Mortensen and Professor Soren Hvidt. These two professor helped train 8 master’s students in the program.
